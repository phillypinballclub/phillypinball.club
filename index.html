Season Details

How to Join

Show up at the venue during league night! We start playing at 7:30pm, and you can sign up on the spot, at any point in the season.

Philly Pinball Club is a an IFPA-approved league, which means every member will also earn points towards their international pinball world ranking!

Schedule

Our Summer season runs for 8 weeks and begins on July 9th.

No venue-hopping this time around, we're changing it up and moving to weekly meetups on Monday nights at Barcade in Fishtown.

Every Monday at 7:30pm we'll be playing for points in our standard match-play format (more on that here). In addition to league points, our final tournament will be structured as a separate IFPA event and open to everyone who has paid league dues.

Feel free to bring friends and guests to any of our league nights — they'll always get to play for free their first time around.

Final Tournament

If you're a PPC member, you're automatically qualified for the end-of-season tournament. Your scores during the regular season will qualify you for A or B Division where you’ll face players of similar skills in the finals.

Cash and prizes are given for the top finishers in both divisions.

Meeting Format

Our meetings are Match Play, where players will compete against one another in groups of 4. You'll be randomly assigned a group for the night, and will accumulate points based on where you finish in each game (1st through 4th). We'll use those points to calculate your position in the season's Standings, which will determine the Division and seed position you'll play in for the Finals.

About the PPC

Mission

The PPC is a club for everyone. All skill levels are welcome, but we are specifically interested in finding and fostering brand new players, as well as committing to an inclusive and respectful environment.

Competition is involved, but we are most focused on providing fun, camaraderie, and encouragement.

Venue

We meet at Barcade in Fishtown, just a block from the SEPTA Girard Station on the MFL.

Cost

Each season costs $20 to participate. League dues go towards overhead costs, awards, and tournament prizes.

Players are also responsible for the cost of games played during league nights (usually around $5). Support your local operators!

Potential new members and visitors can always play free on their first night.

Contact

Write to us at info@phillypinball.club or subscribe to our mailing list below.

Signing up for our mailing list is the best way to keep posted on all PPC activities. We send out announcements, updates, and league night reminders throughout each season.
