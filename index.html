About the PPC

Join the club

The PPC is a club for everyone. Whether you're looking for good competition, or just a low key time with some excellent people, we've got you covered.

We welcome all skill levels, but we're especially interested in finding beginners and folks who are new to the scene. Our members range from brand new players to seasoned competitors, and we're all out to have fun. Come on out and join us!

Venue

We meet at Barcade in Fishtown, just a block from the SEPTA Girard Station on the MFL.

Cost

League dues for each season are $20, and go towards our overhead costs, awards, and tournament prizes.

Players are also responsible for the cost of games played during league nights (usually around $5). Support your local operators!

Potential new members and visitors can always play free on their first night.

Season Details

How to Join

Show up at the venue during league night! We start playing at 7:30pm, and you can sign up on the spot, at any point in the season.

Philly Pinball Club is a an IFPA-approved league, which means every member will also earn points towards their international pinball world ranking!

Meeting Format

Each meeting follows a Match Play format, where players compete against one another in groups of 4. You'll be randomly grouped with 3 other people for the night, and your group will play a series of 4 games together.

Your goal for each game is to beat as many of your group members as possible. In this format, we don't record any actual game scores — instead, you'll earn points based on where you finish in each game (1st through 4th). We'll use those points to calculate your position in the season's Standings, which ultimately determines your seed position in the Finals.

If you've never played competitively (or never played Match Play format), the best thing you can do is show up for league night! We'll always review the rules for any new folks before we get started, and you'll be paired with other members that will be happy to show you the ropes. By the end of the night you'll have the whole thing down.

Final Tournament

If you're a PPC member, you're automatically qualified for the end-of-season tournament, where we give out cash for the top finishers and ridiculous prizes for everyone. Your scores during the regular season will determine your seeding in the tournament, and the number of strikes you earn will determine whether you end up playing for the A division title or the B division title.

We'll send out more details as the Finals draw closer, but rest assured — it's the most fun night of the season and not one to miss!
