About the PPC

Philly Pinball Club is a city-wide pinball league in Philadelphia, PA.

Mission

The PPC is a club for everyone. All skill levels are welcome, but we are specifically interested in finding and fostering brand new players, as well as committing to an inclusive and respectful environment.

Competition is involved, but we are most focused on providing fun, camaraderie, and encouragement.

Cost

$20 per season and guests play free on their first night. League dues go toward paying for operating costs, awards, and tournament prizes.

Players will also be responsible for the cost of games played during league nights.
